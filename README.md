# Online Retail Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes online retail transactions to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and customer value segments.

The dashboard was built in Power BI using cleaned transactional data and DAX measures to support business decision-making.



## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Excel



## Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
* Segment customers using RFM analysis
* Discover opportunities to improve revenue and retention


## Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned using Power Query.

### Cleaning Steps

* Removed cancelled invoices
* Removed negative quantities
* Removed zero-price transactions
* Standardized data types
* Created revenue calculations
* Prepared data for customer segmentation



## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value
* Revenue Trends
* Product Performance

### Customer Segmentation Dashboard

RFM Analysis:

* Champions
* Loyal Customers
* Potential Loyalists
* At Risk Customers
* Lost Customers



## Key Findings

* Total Revenue exceeded $20 million.
* The United Kingdom generated over 80% of revenue.
* November was the strongest sales month.
* Zinc Willie Winkie Candle Stick was the highest revenue-generating product.
* Customer spending was concentrated among a relatively small group of customers.
* Lost and Potential Loyalist customers represented the largest customer segments.



## Business Recommendations

* Increase retention efforts for At Risk customers.
* Focus marketing campaigns on Potential Loyalists.
* Maintain inventory for top-selling products.
* Reduce dependence on a single geographic market.
* Expand loyalty programs for Champions and Loyal customers.
